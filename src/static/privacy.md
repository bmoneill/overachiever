# Overachiever Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use this website (“Service”). By using the Service, you consent to these practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide

* Account identifiers (Steam ID, Xbox Gamertag)
* Email address (if provided)
* Content you submit (comments, guides)

### b. Information Collected Automatically

* IP address, browser type, device information
* Usage data (pages visited, features used, timestamps)
* Cookies and advertising identifiers

### c. Third-Party Data

* Achievement, library, and gameplay data from linked Steam or Xbox accounts
* Profile metadata (username, avatar)
* Collected via APIs or public profiles, subject to your privacy settings

## 3. How We Use Your Information

* Provide and operate the Service
* Display achievements and game data
* Generate achievement guides
* Improve performance and user experience
* Monitor and prevent abuse
* Communicate with you
* Serve personalized or non-personalized ads (subject to consent where required)

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

* Consent (e.g., linking accounts, non-essential cookies)
* Legitimate interests (improving Service, preventing fraud)
* Compliance with law
* Personalized advertising and non-essential cookies rely on explicit consent where required

## 5. Sharing of Information

* Service providers (hosting, analytics)
* Third-party APIs (Steam, Xbox)
* Advertising partners (Google AdSense)
* Legal authorities if required
* Some disclosures may be considered a “sale” or “sharing” under CCPA

## 6. Data Retention

We keep data as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with law, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion anytime.

## 7. Your Rights

* Access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data
* California residents: know, delete, opt out of sale/sharing, limit sensitive data
* Exercise rights via contact or “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” link

## 8. Account Linking and Control

You control data shared through Steam/Xbox privacy settings. Disconnecting stops future collection but does not remove stored data unless requested.

## 9. Consent Management

We use a consent management platform to obtain consent for non-essential cookies and allow you to manage or withdraw consent anytime.

* Opt out: Google: https://www.google.com/settings/ads
